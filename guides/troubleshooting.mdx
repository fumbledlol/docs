## Discord integration not showing data properly

If you encounter any issues, such as that your status is not being shown correctly, or your activity status is not showing up, make sure you have enabled Activity status from the server's privacy settings.

## My profile is not updating

Make sure you refresh your profile a few times after doing changes. This usually happens because you are being served old data before new data is propagated to your profile due to caching.

## I get 403 errors saying "Request forbidden by administrative rules."

Your request has been blocked by our security filters. If you believe this is an error, please contact support with your IP address and the time of the incident.
