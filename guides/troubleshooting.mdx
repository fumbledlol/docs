## Discord integration not showing data properly

If you encounter any issues, such as that your status is not being shown correctly, or your activity status is not showing up, make sure you have enabled Activity status from the server's privacy settings.

## My profile is not updating immediately

Make sure you refresh your profile a few times after doing changes. Updates may take a short moment to appear because cached (stored) data can temporarily show the previous version of your profile.

## I get 403 errors saying "Request forbidden by administrative rules."

Your request has been blocked by our security filters. If this is not occasional and won't go away, your IP is likely blocked by us.

You can try some of these to fix the error:

- Check your browser extensions if there is any extension that may be interfering with your requests.
- Use another network connection

If you believe this is an error, please contact support with your IP address and the time of the incident.
